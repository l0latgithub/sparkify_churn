# Customer churn prediction

## Introduction

Churn is the enemy of any subscription company. Churn is broadly defined as percentage of subscribers who discontinue their service in a given time period.
Churn prediction is an important application of big data and machine learning. Successful churn prediction would allow us to take actions to keep the customers who are going to leave.
